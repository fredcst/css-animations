Small history of CSS animations

Este párrafo se mostrará con una animación al hacer scroll hacia él.

The history of CSS animation dates back to the early days of the web. Primarily used as a means of adding visual appeal to static websites. As technology progressed and javascript, jquery and other libraries were developed, CSS animations evolved and became increasingly dynamic, responding to user interactions.

Today, CSS animations are a staple of modern web and mobile design, enhancing user engagement and elevating the visual experience. Among the various types of scroll animation, horizontal scroll animation stands out for its unconventional approach.

Horizontal scrolling is a matter of debate in the realm of user experience and interface design, with opinions divided on its effectiveness. However, when employed judiciously, such as for catalogs, navigation of large elements, or concealment of secondary content, it can prove to be a valuable tool in the designer's arsenal.

FedecaM, in its pursuit of creating a website that delivers an exceptional user experience, employ horizontal scrolling as a means to demonstrate its effective utilization as a design tool.

About FedecaM

FedecaM is a tutorial website created by students for students. Our goal is to provide an easy-to-follow tutorial on the topic of scroll animation. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics of scroll animation or an experienced learner seeking for inspiration, this website is tailored for you.

FedecaM was largely inspired to follow the trend of Ultimate Simplification. Minimalistic, sleek, and presenting a tiny amount of text content. The idea behind ultra-minimalism is to create a sense of luxury, whilst focusing the user’s attention on what is truly required.

Why Horizontal scrolling? Vertical scrolling is the navigational norm, and the user can be quite easily bored scrolling down an ultra-minimalistic website. FedecaM believes that breaking the norms to bring new to the user is a way to keep our users interested.

This tutorial for horizontal scroll animation is designed to be easy to follow while still providing a lot of value. Walk through each step of the process and create more advanced transitions.
